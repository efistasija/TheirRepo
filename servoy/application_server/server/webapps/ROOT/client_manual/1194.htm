Finding data in related fields

To find related data:

  • Choose Select > Find or press CTRL-F / CMD-F.
  • Click inside a related field.
  • Enter your find criteria.
  • Press Enter or F3 to perform the find.

All related rows (records) containing your criteria will be searched and the matching master rows will be displayed.
